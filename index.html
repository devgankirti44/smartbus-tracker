🏫 About SmartBus

The story behind campus transportation revolution

Transforming Campus Commute

SmartBus was born from a simple frustration — students standing at bus stops with no idea when their bus would arrive. Our team of engineering students built the first prototype in a hackathon, and it quickly grew into a full campus solution.

Today, SmartBus tracks over 50 buses across 200+ routes, serving more than 10,000 students daily. Our system uses real-time GPS, machine learning for ETA prediction, and instant push notifications to ensure no student ever misses their ride.

We partner with the campus transportation department to continuously improve routes, optimize schedules, and reduce wait times. Our mission is to make campus commuting stress-free, efficient, and sustainable.

❓ FAQ

Frequently asked questions about SmartBus

How accurate is the real-time tracking?+
Our GPS tracking updates every 5 seconds with accuracy within 3 meters. We use multi-satellite triangulation and dead reckoning to maintain accuracy even in areas with poor GPS signal like underpasses and tunnels.
What happens if my bus is cancelled?+
You'll receive an instant push notification with alternative bus options and their ETAs. The app automatically suggests the fastest alternative route to your destination. Cancellations are updated in real-time by the transport office.
Is SmartBus available as a mobile app?+
Yes! SmartBus is available on both iOS and Android. You can also use the Progressive Web App (PWA) directly from your browser. The mobile app supports offline schedule viewing and background notifications.
How do I report an issue with a bus or driver?+
Use the "Report Issue" button on any bus tracking page, or visit the Contact section below. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours by the campus transportation committee. Anonymous reporting is also available.
Can I set up alerts for specific routes?+
Absolutely! Go to your profile settings and select "My Routes." You can set custom alerts for departure reminders, delay notifications, and even when your bus is a certain number of minutes away from your stop.
Is the service free for students?+
Yes, SmartBus is completely free for all registered students and faculty members. Simply sign up with your college email ID to get full access. The service is funded by the campus transportation budget.
